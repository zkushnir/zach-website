Zachary Kushnir

Robotics & Neuro-Mechatronics
Pittsburgh, PA · Carnegie Mellon University

I’m a Mechanical Engineering master’s student at Carnegie Mellon University specializing in robotics and neuro-mechatronics. My current research focuses on designing a hand exoskeleton to help restore motor function after stroke and spinal cord injury.

I care about robotics that serve real people: assistive devices, prosthetics, and human–robot interaction grounded in solid mechanics, control, and machine learning.

Outside the lab, I compete in Olympic-style karate for Team USA — the same discipline and resilience that drive my training shape how I approach engineering and research.
